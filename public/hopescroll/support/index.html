Frequently Asked Questions

How do I bookmark a quote?

Tap the heart icon at the bottom of any quote, or double-tap the quote itself to bookmark it instantly. You'll see a heart animation confirming your bookmark.

Where are my bookmarks stored?

All bookmarks are stored locally on your device using iOS secure storage. Your bookmarks never leave your device and are completely private.

How do I view my bookmarked quotes?

Tap the "Bookmarks" tab (heart icon) at the bottom of the screen to see all your saved quotes.

How do I search for specific quotes?

Tap the "Search" tab (magnifying glass) and enter keywords or author names. You can search through thousands of inspirational quotes instantly.

How do I share a quote?

Tap the share icon (square with arrow) at the bottom of any quote. You can share via Messages, social media, or save as an image.

How does the infinite scroll work?

Simply swipe up to see more quotes. The app automatically loads new inspirational content as you scroll, so you never run out of wisdom to explore.

Can I delete a bookmark?

Yes! Tap the heart icon again on any bookmarked quote to remove it from your collection. You can also manage bookmarks from the Bookmarks tab.

Does the app work offline?

The app includes built-in quotes that work offline. For the full collection of quotes, you'll need an internet connection to fetch new content from our quote API.

Do you collect my data or track me?

No. HopeScroll does not collect any personal information, track your usage, or serve ads. See our Privacy Policy for details.

How do I reset the app?

To clear all bookmarks and start fresh, delete and reinstall the app. All data is stored locally, so reinstalling gives you a clean slate.

What iOS version do I need?

HopeScroll requires iOS 18.0 or later.

How often are new quotes added?

The app fetches fresh quotes from our API regularly. Pull down to refresh at the top of the feed to get the latest inspirational content.
