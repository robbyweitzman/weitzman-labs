HopeScroll Privacy Policy

Weitzman Labs LLC

Last Updated: November 8, 2025

Introduction

HopeScroll ("we," "our," or "the app"), developed and published by Weitzman Labs LLC, is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our iOS application.

Information We Collect

We DO NOT Collect:

  • Personal information (name, email, phone number, etc.)
  • Location data
  • Device identifiers for tracking
  • Usage analytics or behavioral data
  • Contacts or photos
  • Any personally identifiable information

What Happens in the App:

Local Bookmark Storage:

  • When you bookmark quotes, this data is stored locally on your device using iOS UserDefaults
  • Bookmark data never leaves your device
  • We have no access to your bookmarks

Network Usage:

  • The app fetches inspirational quotes from ZenQuotes API (a third-party service)
  • These network requests contain no personal information
  • We do not track which quotes you view

Third-Party Services

ZenQuotes API

We use ZenQuotes API (zenquotes.io) to provide inspirational quote content. When you use the app:

  • Quote requests are sent to ZenQuotes servers
  • No personal information is included in these requests
  • Please review ZenQuotes' privacy policy at: https://zenquotes.io/privacy-policy

No Analytics or Tracking

  • We do not use analytics services
  • We do not use advertising networks
  • We do not track your behavior
  • We do not create user profiles

Data Storage and Security

  • All bookmark data is stored locally on your device using secure iOS UserDefaults
  • We have no servers or databases that store user data
  • Your bookmarks are not synchronized to iCloud (in this version)
  • Deleting the app will remove all locally stored data

Children's Privacy

HopeScroll is suitable for users of all ages. We do not knowingly collect any information from children or adults.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in the "Last Updated" date above.

Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at:

  • Email: support@weitzmanlabs.com

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Use the app anonymously (no account required)
  • Delete all locally stored data by deleting the app
  • Not have your data tracked, sold, or shared

Compliance

This app complies with:

  • Apple App Store Guidelines
  • iOS Privacy Requirements
  • General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) principles
  • California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA) principles
Summary: HopeScroll does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information. Your bookmarks stay on your device. We use a third-party API for quote content, but send no identifying information.
